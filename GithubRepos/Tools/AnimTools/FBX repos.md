## FBX Anim Crop Tools
[J-Andersen-UvA/FBXAnimCropTools](https://github.com/J-Andersen-UvA/FBXAnimCropTools)
We collected many body and face animations but we were not able to re-synchronize them after due to wanting the original body animation and not the retargeted body animation for use on the new avatar. We used this tool to extract the moment of a foot pedal press, and we aligned them in post to get a complete animation again.
