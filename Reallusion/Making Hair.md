Making Hair exist out of 2 steps:
1. Creating the texture of the hair
2. Creating the 3D model of the hair

Most tutorials do this in Blender.

The 3D model is usually made of hair cards, which are strips of meshes that get turned into hair by using the texture.
The following videos show how to make the texture and hair cards:
[How to Create CC Smart Hair Part 1 | Character Creator 4 Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=RSzXXbXBXlw&t=3s)
